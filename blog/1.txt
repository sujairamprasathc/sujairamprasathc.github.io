Hello World,
So I decided to start my own website and post something in it. Pretty much
anything I think of doing, my mind gives me a thousand reasons against it.
    You don't know html  
    You don't know CSS   
    The website looks bad  
    You don't even have any idea as to what to do with this  
    So on and so forth  
But, I am going to go forward and post something for there is one thing I
learnt from going mountain after mountain during this lockdown. It is not the
view at the top of the mountain that is pretty. Rather, the view at the top
sucks. A bunch of monotonic building on this side of the city and quarries on
the other side. Granted, a few mountains do have stunning viewpoints. But it
is not always guaranteed to find amazing scenery at the top. Rather, it is the
little beauty here and there scattered throughout the mountain, that keeps me
going. The same way, although a fully finished website would be great; it is
the whole process of building from scratch that gives me happiness. And thus
it begins. My own website; purely experimental.
